~/about_me

My name is Lucia Escanellas, also known as raviolicode and I live in Amsterdam.

Engineer by profession, coder by choice, I believe in how doing "the right things the right way" can make a difference.

I love solving problems in all their forms. Setting up servers, defining architectures, coaching teams in agile methodologies, coding a usable front-end, automating whenever possible.

But above all, seeing a project's success is what really drives me.

I have been fascinated by computers since I was 9, when there was only Xenix and DOS.

Started out as a Linux Sysadmin, and then a computer networks teacher at the University.

I also worked as a .NET Developer / Analyst. Last six years have been all about Linux, and I have been working on a variety of Front-End, Back-End and Infrastructure.
